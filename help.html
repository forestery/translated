How to use
  • You can press "Ctrl+Shift+F" to open this extension.
  • Select a word on web ,then just press "Ctrl+Shift+F" to translate the word to chinese.
  • Type a word in the input field and press Enter key.
  • In the new word list field, click the word to translate, double click the word to remove it when you know it.
